About Me

After graduating with a business degree, I built and scaled multiple e-commerce ventures from scratch, handling everything from development to digital marketing. This led me to expand into content creation, where I grew a successful YouTube channel while managing video production for various clients.

Through these entrepreneurial experiences, I consistently encountered technical limitations that prevented me from fully realizing my product visions. I'm excited to bring my unique blend of business acumen, marketing expertise, and engineering skills to a product team that values innovation.
